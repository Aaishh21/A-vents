About Us

Connecting communities through culture, creativity, and shared experiences

Our Mission

“A-vents” was created to bridge the gap between amazing cultural experiences and the people who seek them. We believe that art, music, and community events have the power to bring people together, spark creativity, and enrich lives.

Whether you're a longtime resident or just visiting Atyrau, our platform makes it easy to discover concerts, exhibitions, festivals, and cultural events that match your interests and schedule.
